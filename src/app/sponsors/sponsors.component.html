Our Five Level Sponsorship Program

We have a Five Level Sponsorship program in which each level has a different set of benefits, and in which all rewards per level are included in the next level up. Depending on how much your company donates in money, goods, and/or services, you will be assigned to a different level.

  • Level One: Up to $499
    • Name on the BR website and social media
  • Level Two: $500–$1499
    • Name on BR website and social media
    • Logo and company name on our t-shirts
  • Level Three: $1500–$2999
    • Name on BR website and social media
    • Medium-sized logo on our t-shirts
    • Logo on team banner
  • Level Four: $3000–$4999
    • Name on BR website and social media
    • Large logo on t-shirts and robot
    • Logo on team banner
    • Invitation to speak at the BR end of the year banquet
  • Level Five: $5000+
    • Logo and name on BR website and social media
    • Large logo on t-shirts and robot
    • Logo on team banner
    • Invitation to speak at the BR end of the year banquet
    • Whenever it is able to, BR will mention sponsors at events and competitions. The team and the company will determine a special event or activity to honor the contribution made.
