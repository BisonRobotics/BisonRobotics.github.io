Bison Robotics

Bison Robotics' goal is to channel the energy and enthusiasm elicited by robotics through the rigorous process of engineering design. Members will apply engineering knowledge and principles in a structured approach to design and build competetive robots. Check out our Discord!

Bison Robotics is comprised of 5 projects and multiple volunteering opportunities. Bison Robotics volunteers at the 5 FIRST Robotics Competition regional events, both the FIRST Lego League and Tech Challenge tournaments here at NDSU, and at multiple VEX competitions. We volunteer to be able to inspire younger people to pursue an interest in the STEM fields.
