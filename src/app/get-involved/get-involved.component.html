Interested in Sponsoring a Project?

Want to get involved, but you are not a student at NDSU? Help sponsor one of Bison Robotics' projects through an in-kind or monetary donation! Sponsorship of any measure is what allows Bison Robotics to successfully participate in all of its engineering challenges; therefore, it is appreciated on a colossal level. If you are interested in sponsoring the organization in any way, please visit the sponsor page for more information.
